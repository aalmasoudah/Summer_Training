*   **The Deficit:** To lose weight, you must eat less than you burn. A deficit of **500 calories per day** usually results in 0.5 kg loss per week.

**2. Food Quality Rules**
*   **Prioritize Protein:** Aim for 1.6g to 2g of protein per kg of body weight (or at least 30g per meal). Protein keeps you full and preserves muscle mass.
*   **Volume Eating:** Eat plenty of vegetables (leafy greens, broccoli, peppers) to fill your stomach without adding many calories.
*   **Minimize "Ultra-Processed" Foods:** Limit sugary drinks, white bread, pastries, and processed snacks. These are often high in "empty calories."
*   **Hydration:** Drink 3–4 liters of water daily. Sometimes thirst is mistaken for hunger.
